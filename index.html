About Me

Currently, I am a Lecturer in School of Information and Communication Technology in Griffith University. I received my PhD degree in The University of Queensland (UQ), Australia in 2014. Between Jan 2014 and July 2016, I have been an ARC post-doctoral research fellow supervised by Prof. Xue Li in Data and Knowledge Engineering(DKE) Group in School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering in UQ. From July 2016, I started to serve Griffith University and Big Data and Smart Analytics Lab, led by Prof. Bela Stantic, as a Lecturer of Computer Science.

My research interest includes various topics of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, and Data Mining. Also, I have always been concentrating on significant applications using AI and Data Mining techniques, such as big medical data analysis and social media analysis.
